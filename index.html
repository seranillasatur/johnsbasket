About Us

The name of the business is derived from the words “Juan”, which is the national personification of the Philippines but westernized as “John”, and “Bayongs” which are bags made from dried leaves in the Philippines but also translated into English as “Basket”. This is because the business is selling American products that are marketed for local Filipinos. Additionally, the word “Basket” is used because the business aims to be somewhat of a ‘personal shopper’ for customers to lessen their problems in meal planning.


.....but how do we achieve that? With meal kits!


Meal kits are pre-planned meals curated by our experts; but with personalization as you can choose what meals you want to eat over the course of a week. Then, our menu refreshes options to ensure that you will never get tired of eating the same food over and over!


You can also choose whether you want your meal kits deconstructed or frozen. The former lets you cook for yourself and your family while the latter is even more stress-free, you just need to re-heat! With John's Basket, meal planning just got a lot more fun!

How It Works

1.  Choose your Meals

Choose from our easy 30-minute weekly recipes, available in frozen or deconstructed.

2.  Receive your bayong!

We deliver fresh organic ingredients for your favorite meals in a cool insulated box right to your door.

3.  Cook, eat, and enjoy!

The old “what do you want to eat?” conversation is about to be banished from your life. Simply follow the instructions included or just re-heat!
